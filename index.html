Healthy roots nourish a community

Jesyka Ricker — IBCLC

My name is Jesyka Ricker and I am fiercely passionate about accessible and compassionate support for nursing parents and their children.

Nursing my son sparked my scientific love of lactation leading me to get a master’s degree in human lactation, become an Internationally Board Certified Lactation Consultant (IBCLC), and founding Ancient Roots Lactation, LLC.

My master’s thesis was on the support needed from healthcare providers for parents to meet the recommended 2 years of providing human milk to their children. Improving self-advocacy and self-confidence for every client is always one of my main goals.

I believe every parent deserves access to evidence-based educational resources to make informed decisions that are best for them and their families. By working to connect healthcare providers and families with necessary resources, I hope to benefit parents, their children, and the community we all share.

How it works

Common Reasons for Visits:

  • Latch concerns/pain with latch
  • Milk transfer concerns
  • Pump and bottle introduction
  • Back-to-work
  • Plugged ducts/Mastitis
  • Breast and/or nipple pain
  • Oversupply
  • Undersupply/Increasing supply
  • Solids Introduction
  • Teething
  • Weaning

All visits are home visits and include:

  • Medical history evaluation
  • Physical breast evaluation of mother
  • Oral evaluation of infant(s) if present
  • Weight check of the infant(s) if present
  • Evidence-based support and education
  • Detailed care plan
  • Itemized superbill to submit to insurance for reimbursement
  • Text or phone support to track care plan results and answer questions
  • Resource/Educational slides for your reference whenever
